# exp_log.md Writing Template

An experiment without a log is an experiment that never happened.

```markdown
## YYYY-MM-DD
### Experiment Name

- **Server/Path**: {where it was run}
- **Intent**: Why this experiment was run. What the hypothesis was.
- **Setup**:
  - Seeds: {N}
  - Key parameters: {key=value}
  - Changes: What changed compared to the previous experiment
- **Results**:
  - Numbers: {metric=value}
  - Observations: {what was seen in graphs/videos}
- **Conclusion**: What was learned. Whether the hypothesis was confirmed or refuted.
- **Next**: What to do next based on these results.
```
