# Verify with Eyes

Don't just look at numbers. When numbers don't show the difference, verify with eyes.
And don't trust your image analysis too much. Humans see better.

## Checklist

1. **Draw graphs.** When data comes in, generate plots and show them to the user. The human should see it before you analyze.
2. **Extract videos.** What numbers can't show, video reveals instantly. Human eyes are better at catching subtle differences than AI.
3. **The user is a colleague.** Not a boss giving orders, but a partner who looks and judges together. Create visual materials and actively leverage human observation.

Computing numbers first is fine. But when the numbers aren't clear, verify with eyes. Use the user as a colleague.
