# Go Back to Fundamentals

When you're stuck, go back to the bottom. Don't be afraid to start over.
Rebuilding from the foundation with your experience makes it stronger than the first time. Don't build castles on sand.

## Checklist

1. **When lost, go down.** If nothing you fix at the top works, the foundation is shaky. Go back to basics and verify from there.
2. **Ask "why?"** If you can't explain the reason, you don't understand it. "That's just how it's done" is not an answer.
3. **Going back is not going backwards.** Once you've climbed up and come back down, you see the terrain. You'll climb again faster and more accurately than the first time.
